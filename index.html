Private Communication, Reimagined

UnknownCrypt is a next-generation, open-source communication platform designed for maximum privacy and anonymity. No tracking. No identity. No compromise.

Core Features

🔐 End-to-End Encryption

Messages are encrypted on your device and can only be read by the receiver.

🆔 Anonymous Identity

No phone numbers, emails, or personal data required. You remain anonymous.

🚫 Zero Tracking

No logs, no analytics, and no metadata storage. Your activity stays private.

💥 Self-Destruct Messages

Messages disappear automatically after being read or after a set time.

🌐 Secure Relay Network

Messages are routed through secure relays to protect your identity.

🧬 Open Source

Fully transparent. Anyone can audit, verify, and contribute to the system.

Built for Transparency

UnknownCrypt is fully open-source. This means anyone can inspect the code, verify the security, and contribute improvements.

Our Mission

To create a communication system where users have complete control over their privacy, identity, and data. Privacy is not a feature — it is the foundation.
